Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09.09.2025

Our mobile games are created by Fun Oak Games. We respect children’s privacy and comply with data protection regulations (COPPA, GDPR-K).

1. Personal Data

The Game does not collect or store any personal data from children or users.

2. Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase)

To improve the quality of the Game, we use Google Analytics for Firebase, which collects anonymous statistical data. This data may include, for example:

  • device type
  • operating system version
  • time spent in the app
  • actions performed in the game
  • crash reports and performance data

The information is used only to analyze and improve the quality of the app. This data cannot identify your child.

3. Children under 13

The Game is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that such data has been provided, it will be deleted immediately.

4. In-App Purchases

The Game offers a one-time purchase (“unlock all levels”):

  • The purchase is available only in the Parent Panel, protected by parental control.
  • Payments are processed through Google Play and require adult confirmation.

5. Ads

The Game does not display ads.
